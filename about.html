About Our Club

Membership

All residents become immediate free members and their property number becomes their membership number (Sanctuary Mews SM 1–73 and Sanctuary Court SC 1–43). You decide how much of your personal information (name, email, phone numbers) you make available to other residents.

Guests

Members may bring their guests to the clubhouse and guests are welcome where applicable to join our activities and events.
