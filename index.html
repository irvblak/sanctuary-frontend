Payments for chargeable events will be made by bank transfer to a single club account or, where agreed, by cash directly to the event host. Full details will be added here once the club account arrangements are finalised.

Contacting the committee

A small group of residents will act as the Sanctuary Club committee. Contact details and responsibilities will be listed in the members area once the system is in regular use.

Members & contact details

The members directory will allow residents to share, if they wish, their name, email address and (optionally) phone number, together with a simple property code such as SM12 or SC07.

This directory will be visible only to Sanctuary Club members and will respect individual privacy preferences. It is intended to make it easier for neighbours to stay in touch socially and around club activities.
